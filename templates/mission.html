Our Perspective on the Social Challenge

How this dashboard addresses preventable infectious diseases

Preventable infectious diseases continue to claim millions of lives each year, disproportionately affecting communities with limited access to healthcare and vaccination programmes. Our dashboard addresses this challenge by making WHO immunisation data freely accessible, transparent, and easy to explore for every stakeholder—from policymakers to the general public.

Transparency

Present unbiased, source-verified vaccination and infection data.

Accessibility

Designed for diverse users—from health officials to curious citizens.

Action

Enable evidence-based decisions that improve global immunisation outcomes.
